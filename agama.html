The Holy Family Church and Community Service

The Holy Family Catholic Church is a church with a unique architectural design, located in New Taipei City, Taiwan. It's structure has a shape of a rectangle and a cross, inside the church has a large altar, many benches, statues and stained glasses windows. The church is also surrounded by a beautiful garden, making it a peaceful place for worship and reflection.

In the Community Service, we helped the Tzu Chi Foundation to clean, organize trash. There were a lot of trash, ranging from bottles, plastics bags, cans to bicycles and metals.
