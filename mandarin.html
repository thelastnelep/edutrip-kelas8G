Stella Maris Ursuline High School

Stella Maris Ursuline High School is the school I visited at the Edutrip. This school is located in Hualien, Taiwan. This school is a big school with 2 basketball, volleyball courts, a running track and 3 big buildings.

The people in Stella Maris Ursuline High School are very friendly, welcoming and kind. They introduced us to their school and teached us many things like physics, kabaddi, games, cooking and other stuff. Their daily activities is going to school in the morning with 1-2 lessons until the first break, then 2 lessons, then they have lunch and eat from the cafeteria, which is filled with healthy food, after that, they have 2 more lessons and finish school at 4 PM. They then usually go home and do their homework, or go to the gym, or do something else.
