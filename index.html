Edutrip Taiwan, Hualien

Learn about the Edutrip to Hualien, Taiwan.

Why Visit Hualien?

In this Edutrip, we travelled to Hualien, Hualien is a beautiful place with its breathtaking natural beauty and vibrant culture. Hualien offered breathtaking experiences such as the Qixingtan Scenic Area and Beach, which was known for its amazing view and half-moon shape. The Dongdamen Night Market is a night market with a variety of stalls ranging from Taiwanese food like Wonton and Xiao Long Bao to games like basketball. We tasted delicious Taiwanese cuisine like Xiao Long Bao and Wonton. We also participated in educational activities like making mochi at the Amis Mochi Factory. This city, offered a relaxed, small-town atmosphere with many different astonishing places with the delicious food and charming night markets.

Inside Hualien, we also visited Stella Maris Ursuline High School. It is a really big school with plenty of students, the students and teachers were really friendly and welcoming. Stella Maris Ursuline High School had 2 basketball courts, a volleyball court, a running track and 3 buildings. The students and teachers there teached us about different lessons and activities like physics and learning kabaddi.
