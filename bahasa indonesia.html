Lakeshore Hotel

Lake Shore Hotel is a hotel at No. 142, Jung Mei Rd., Hualien City, Hualien County, Taiwan. The hotel's phone number is 038-222-666, established in 2011 and owned by Duen Mou Foundation of Taiwan. This hotel has a rating of 4.3. This hotel is located in Hualien City, Hualien has many interesting destinations such as Qixingtan Beach which has a half-moon shape and Dongdamen Night Market which has many choices of food and goods. The typical culture and arts in Hualien are mochi and summer festivals. The climate in Hualien is rainfall and the temperature is 23.4 Â°C. The charming thing is like the whispering stone at Qixingtan Beach. The exchange rate is 1 NTD (New Taiwan Dollar) to 500 IDR.

Lakeshore Hotel is located in Hualien, Zhongmei Rd. The facilities and infrastructure of this hotel are gym and restaurant. Inside the gym there are equipment such as dumbbells and jump ropes, inside the restaurant there are foods with many choices such as chicken, rice and bread. Inside the Lakeshore hotel, there is a FamilyMart to buy food and drinks. Inside the Lakeshore hotel there are several room variants, namely deluxe king and twin, this room has 1 and 2 beds, a bathroom with a bathtub and the price is 2720 NTD. After that, there is a premier king room with sea and mountain views which is like a deluxe king and twin, but there is a bathtub near the window, it is bigger and the price is 3040 NTD for a premier king with a mountain view and 3520 NTD for a premier king with a sea view. Finally, the Lakeshore Suite is the most expensive room at 4720 NTD, has a sea view, lots of decorations and is bigger than other room variants.

Reviews The advantages of this hotel are, the Lakeshore hotel rooms are very good. The rooms feel clean, tidy and safe. Inside there is a bathroom with a bathtub, a large bed and the room is big. The hotel restaurant has many choices of delicious food such as eggs, chicken, etc.

The downside of this hotel is that the room is expensive at 2720 NTD and the room walls are not soundproof and you can hear the next room. The bathroom door lock is not good, because the key can lock itself and you can't get into the bathroom.

The conclusion of this review is, Lakeshore hotel is a good hotel to stay while traveling in Hualien because this hotel is not far from tourist attractions such as Dongdamen night market and Qixingtan beach.

In addition, the Lakeshore Hotel offers a serene and relaxing environment, making it an ideal place for families, couples, and solo travelers. Its proximity to nature and modern amenities ensures a perfect balance between comfort and adventure.

I recommend staying at this hotel because, the room is very safe and good with facilities such as a restaurant that has delicious food and a good gym.
