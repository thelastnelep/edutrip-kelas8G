Culture in Taiwan

Culture in Taiwan is unique as several cultures are blended together. The culture of Taiwan is a mix of traditional Chinese culture, indigenous culture, and modern influences from Japan and the West. The island's history has shaped its cultural landscape, making it a vibrant and diverse place to explore.

Traditions and festivals are also part of Taiwan's culture, with festivals like the Lantern Festival and Dragon Boat Festival celebrated with great enthusiasm. The island is also known for its delicious street food, night markets, and tea culture.
